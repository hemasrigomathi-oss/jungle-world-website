WILDLIFE SANCTUARY

Anaimalai Tiger Reserve

It is a beautiful wildlife habitat in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu. It is home to tigers, elephants, leopards, lion-tailed macaques, and many bird species, making it one of India's richest biodiversity hotspots

Mundanthurai Wildlife Sanctuary

It is a lush forest sanctuary in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu. It is home to tigers, leopards, elephants, Nilgiri langurs, and many rare birds, making it an important center for wildlife conservation and biodiversity.

Mudumalai Wildlife Sanctuary

It is one of India's oldest wildlife sanctuaries, located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu. It is famous for its tigers, elephants, leopards, deer, and rich forest ecosystems, making it a vital habitat for wildlife conservation.

Nellai Wildlife Sanctuary

It is a protected forest area in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. It provides a safe habitat for deer, monkeys, birds, and various native wildlife species, helping to conserve the region's rich biodiversity and natural ecosystems.

Point Calimere Wildlife Sanctuary

It is a unique coastal sanctuary in Tamil Nadu, known for its wetlands, mangrove forests, and rich birdlife. It is home to blackbucks, flamingos, pelicans, and many migratory birds, making it an important wildlife and bird conservation area.

Vallanadu Blackbuck Sanctuary

It is a protected wildlife area in Tamil Nadu famous for its population of blackbucks. The sanctuary also supports foxes, hares, peafowl, and other grassland species, helping preserve the region's unique biodiversity.
