BIRDS SANCTUARY

Vedanthangal Bird Sanctuary

It is one of India's oldest and most famous bird sanctuaries. It is home to thousands of migratory and resident birds, including storks, herons, egrets, and pelicans, making it a paradise for bird lovers and nature enthusiasts.

Pulicat Bird Sanctuary

It is a beautiful coastal wetland known for its large flocks of migratory birds. It is famous for flamingos, pelicans, ducks, and many other waterbirds, making it one of South India's most important bird habitats

Vaduvoor Bird Sanctuary

It is a popular wetland sanctuary in Tamil Nadu that attracts thousands of migratory birds each year. It is known for pelicans, ducks, herons, egrets, and other waterbirds, making it a vibrant destination for birdwatching and nature conservation.

Karikili Bird Sanctuary

It is a peaceful wetland habitat in Tamil Nadu that supports a wide variety of migratory and resident birds. It is famous for storks, herons, egrets, ibises, and other waterbirds, making it an important site for bird conservation and birdwatching.

Koonthankulam Bird Sanctuary

It is one of Tamil Nadu's largest bird sanctuaries, attracting thousands of migratory birds every year. It is famous for pelicans, painted storks, herons, egrets, and many other waterbirds, making it a major center for bird conservation and nesting.

Kazhuveli Bird Sanctuary

It is a vast wetland ecosystem in Tamil Nadu that provides a vital habitat for migratory and resident birds. It is known for flamingos, pelicans, herons, egrets, and other waterbirds, making it an important destination for bird conservation and nature enthusiasts.
